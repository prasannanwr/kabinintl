Meet our Team

Our team comprises of professionals with vast and in-depth experience in various fields. The company's objective is to synergize these expertise to serve our clients better through one-stop shop.
The resources in the Associates’ team bring in over three decades of professional experience in their respective fields as elaborated in their CVs.

Narendra Prajapati

Mr. Prajapati is an MBA – Finance, degree holder from TU, Nepal (1987). He also holds a diploma in Architecture from IoE, TU, Nepal (1981). Mr. Prajapati is currently the MD of N.P. Associates Pvt. Ltd (NPA) – an Advisory, Consultancy and Management Support services providing company. He has over four decades of experiences in Technical and Management field in academics, projects, operations, and advisory services. During last 15 years, he was engaged extensively in Hydropower and related fields including as a CEO of an IPP – Bhote Koshi Power Company Pvt. Ltd. His company with its team of associates recently concluded seamless services to their client - Stantec Consulting International LLC, for their MCA-Nepal ETP PPTS works in Nepal office operations. He brings in additional experiences from hospitality, real estate and academics of over 2 decades.
He had also served as an EC Member in Independent Power Producer Association of Nepal (IPPAN).

Rajeev Sharma

Mr. Sharma acquired his B.E. (Electrical) degree with specialization in Power System in 1984 from the Regional Engineering College, Srinagar, Kashmir, India. From December 1984 he served continuously in Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) for 34 years and retired from the post of Deputy Managing Director in November 2018. His 34 years' experience in the power sector involved distribution, transmission, generation, engineering-services as well as planning which includes more than 20 years' experience in the transmission sector alone.
From November 2018 till March 2021 he served as "Senior Technical Advisor (National)" for Stantec LLC for the MCC funded "Project Preparation and Technical Supervision Services" Works for MCA Nepal's Electricity Transmission Project, which involved survey, investigation and design of 312 km, 400 kV double-circuit, quad-conductor transmission line and associated 400/220/132/33 kV substations.

Sushil Aryal

Mr. Aryal has obtained M.Sc. degree in Human Settlement Development, specializing in rural and regional development planning from Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Thailand, in 1986. He has also completed Degree in Business Administration and Commerce from Tribhuvan University in 1977. He has more than 40 years of research, training and consultancy experience. During last 23 years, he has involved extensively on consultancy in the field of social development, social safeguard, resettlement and rehabilitation of the project affected people in national and international projects. He has worked as a resource person in number of projects funded by the World Bank, Asian Development Bank and MCC. Mr. Aryal has experience of working as a consultant in Afghanistan, Cambodia, Laos and Vietnam. Formerly, he was an Associate Professor (Management) at Center for Economic Development and Administration (CEDA), Tribhuvan University, Nepal.

Ashok Tuladhar

Mr. Tuladhar acquired his Diploma in Statistics (Science) in 1979 from Trichandra Campus, Tribhuwan University, Kathmandu, Nepal and Bachelor in Civil Engineering with the fourth year elective subject Construction Technology in 1985 from University of Roorkee, Roorkee, India. Then served around 30 years in the Nepal Electricity Authority till 2015 retiring as a Deputy Manager (Civil). Apart from full time service to Nepal Electricity Authority, as part timer, he practiced building structural design engaging with private consulting firms in Nepal. In his 35 years’ experience, he engaged his last 21 years in the structural design, review & prototype testing of HV Transmission Line Towers including foundations, the design & review of Substation Civil Works, the transmission line walkover & detail survey along with tower spotting and the construction supervision of civil works. He has also served as Sr TL Engineer in the MCA-Nepal ETP PPTS for Stantec Consulting Intel LLC.
